Nominate a KAUST alumnus who is making an impact for the 2022 KAUST Alumni Change Makers Awards.

The KAUST Alumni Change Makers Awards recognize the impact being made by alumni in Saudi Arabia and internationally. The annual awards, to be launched in 2022, share and celebrate the impact of MS and PhD graduates within the global KAUST alumni community.
You can be a part of this celebration by nominating impactful and inspirational alumni, whether those alumni are your former students, fellow alumni or your colleagues.

The 3 categories are:

1- Leadership and Impact
2- Research, Innovation & Entrepreneurship
3- Social Impact

Online nomination

Please submit the nomination form by midnight on Saturday June 4, 2022.
