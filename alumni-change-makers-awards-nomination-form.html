Nomination Form

Tell us about your nominee!

Please follow the nomination requirements below and fill out the form below if your candidate qualifies.

Your nominee has graduated from KAUST with a MS or Ph.D.

You have sought approval from the alumnus to nominate them for this award.

Self-nominations will not be accepted.

The same alumnus can be nominated for each of the three categories, but a separate nomination form and criteria is required for each.

If more than one nomination in the same category is received for an individual, only one nomination forms will be considered.

All eligible nominations will be reviewed by a selection committee.

Award recipients will be acknowledged in the media and via University communication channels, including the KAUST website and KAUST official social media.

Alumni Affairs will liaise directly with the nominator of the alumnus to advise on outcome.
